Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 February 2025

At PEARMIACT, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, services, and products.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email, phone number, billing/shipping address.
  • Payment details: processed securely via third-party payment providers.
  • Account login information (if you create an account).
  • Browsing data: cookies, device information, IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process orders and deliver products.
  • Provide tutoring services and schedule sessions.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website, products, and services.
  • Send relevant updates, offers, and newsletters (if you opt in).

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with:

  • Trusted third parties such as payment processors, couriers, and service providers.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and track site performance. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at: privacy@pearmiact.co.za
